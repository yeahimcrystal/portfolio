The internet works in 4 steps. It starts from User1 creating the data. The data gets recieved by the TCP. The IP breaks it down into smaller "packets". It goes into User1's hardware and gets sent to the Internet cloud. User2's computer recieves the data in the hardware. The IP puts it back together and the TCP recieves it.
